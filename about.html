About Me

Founder and Writer of Tech 4 Our Future

Hey there! My name is Vasanthavel Jeeva Kumararaja but you can call me Jeeva for short. I was born in Kuwait and immigrated to the United States when I was two years old. I was born to Indian parents and I am very proud of my South Asian roots. I am currently a senior living in New York City and apsire to major in Computer Engineering.


My purpose for creating Tech 4 Our Future is to blend my interests of exploring technology and fighting climate change in a variety of blog posts to create educational content for users. My goal for my personal blog is to inform young people about the latest technologies which have the potential to create large impacts on the world and will help us reach our goal of net-zero carbon emissions in the near future.


This blog couldn't have been created if it weren't for the skills I gained in AllStarCode (learning how to code my own blog) and my experience as an intern for Rockaway Initiative for Sustainability which fostered my interest in protecting our planet and fighting against climate change.
